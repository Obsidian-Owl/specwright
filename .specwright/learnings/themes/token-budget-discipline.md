# Token Budget Discipline

Most common friction source across work units. SKILL.md files and protocols regularly exceed word/token budgets during implementation, requiring revision passes.

Key findings: measure baselines before editing (P16), tighten preemptively at 80% budget (P2), use structural rewrites for protocols rather than word trimming (P15), fix pre-existing overages when surfaced (P13).

Trajectory is positive — each unit refines the approach from reactive trimming to proactive measurement.
